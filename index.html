About Me

Ming Li received the M.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Henan Normal University (Xinxiang, China) in 2024, supervised by Prof. Peiyan Yuan .

From August 2024 to June 2025, she worked at xFusion Digital Co., Ltd. & Zhengzhou College of Finance and Economics (Zhengzhou, China).

Currently, she is pursuing the Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Engineering at Xidian University (Xi’an, China), under the supervision of Prof. Nan Cheng , and is a member of the ✨Ubiquitous Networking and Intelligent Computing Lab (UNIC).

🔍 Main Research Interests

  • Generative AI: Focus on signal processing and practical scenario implementation
  • Decision-Making AI: Lyapunov-based Deep Reinforcement Learning for resource allocation in adaptive communication scenarios
        (Targeting 🚗 V2X Networks / 🛰️ Satellite Communication / ✈️ Aeronautical Communication / 📶 Mobile Communication)
  • AI-Driven Plugin Development for Intelligent Communication Systems: Multi-agent resource scheduling, network resource management, and self-optimization
